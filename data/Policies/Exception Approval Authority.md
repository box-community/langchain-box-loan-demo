# Exception Approval Authority
Updated: January 2025

## Policy Violation Levels

### Minor Violations (Manager Approval)
- DTI between 43-45%
- LTV between maximum and +5% over
- Credit score 10 points below minimum
- Employment: 18-24 months (vs 24 required)

### Moderate Violations (Regional Director Approval)
- DTI between 45-48%
- LTV between +5% and +10% over maximum
- Credit score 20 points below minimum
- Two minor violations combined

### Major Violations (Automatic Denial)
- DTI above 48%
- LTV more than +10% over maximum
- Credit score more than 20 points below minimum
- Three or more violations (any level)
- Fraudulent documentation

## Compensating Factors
May allow one violation level down if present:
- Large down payment (20%+)
- Substantial liquid reserves (6+ months payments)
- Co-signer with excellent credit
- Previous auto loan paid successfully
