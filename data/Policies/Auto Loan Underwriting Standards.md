# Auto Loan Underwriting Standards
Version 2.1 | Effective Date: January 2025

## Eligibility Requirements

### Credit Standards
- Minimum credit score: 620
- No bankruptcies in past 24 months
- No auto repossessions in past 36 months
- Maximum open collections: $5,000

### Income Requirements
- Maximum debt-to-income (DTI) ratio: 43%
- Minimum 2 years employment (current employer OR continuous in field)
- Income must be verifiable via pay stubs, tax returns, or bank statements
- Self-employed: 2 years tax returns required

### Collateral Standards
- **New vehicles:** Maximum LTV 120%
- **Used vehicles (0-3 years):** Maximum LTV 115%
- **Used vehicles (4-7 years):** Maximum LTV 110%
- **Vehicles 8+ years:** Maximum LTV 100%

### Loan Terms
- Minimum loan amount: $5,000
- Maximum loan amount: $75,000
- Maximum term: 72 months
- Minimum down payment: 0% for qualified borrowers (700+ credit)

## Risk-Based Pricing
- Tier 1 (740+ credit): Prime rate
- Tier 2 (680-739): Prime + 2%
- Tier 3 (620-679): Prime + 4%
