# Vehicle Valuation Guidelines
Version 1.3 | Updated: Q4 2024

## New Vehicle Valuation
- Use MSRP or dealer invoice (whichever is lower)
- Include destination charges
- Exclude dealer add-ons and accessories

## Used Vehicle Valuation
- Calculate average of: KBB, NADA, Edmunds
- Use "clean" condition unless inspection shows otherwise
- Adjust for mileage relative to age
- Regional market adjustments allowed (Â±5%)

## Depreciation Schedules (Annual %)

### Passenger Cars
- Year 1: 20%
- Year 2: 15%
- Year 3: 10%
- Year 4+: 8%

### SUVs/Trucks
- Year 1: 18%
- Year 2: 12%
- Year 3: 9%
- Year 4+: 7%

### Luxury Vehicles
- Year 1: 25%
- Year 2: 18%
- Year 3: 12%
- Year 4+: 10%

## Minimum Collateral Value
At loan maturity, collateral value must equal or exceed 80% of remaining loan balance.
